About me

Hello! My name is Alexandra Haret and I live in Edinburgh, Scotland.

In my spare time, I work on various side projects, run, drink coffee, cook, fix things and explore new places both in the UK and abroad.

I graduated high school with a major in computer science and math then got my BA in Sociology and MA in Marketing. After having worked for just over a decade as a digital marketing specialist, I decided to embrace a new career as a front end developer in order to make better use of my analytical and creative skills.

As I have always had an interest in technology and web development in particular, I started off by studying the basics (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript) and then moved on to jQuery, AngularJS, Bootstrap, Foundation and more. Over the past years, I've applied my newly acquired knowledge and skills in various projects for external clients, as well as personal projects that challenged me to learn even more technologies.

I currently built websites and web apps with a focus on responsiveness and delightful user experience, following best-practice web standards. My latest projects are listed in the 'Recent work' section above.

Skills and expertise

Front end development

I write code that follows the W3C's web standards and semantic best practices. I use technologies like HTML5, CSS3 and jQuery to transform static artwork into mobile first, pixel prefect, interactive and fully functional websites.
